Revolution is a young adult historical fiction novel by Jennifer Donnelly about a girl named Andi Alpers who is struggling with drugs, thoughts of suicide, and the way her family has fallen apart after the death of her ten-year-old brother The first season started with the airing of the Pilot on NBC on September 17, 2012, and the second with Born in the U.S.A. on September 25, 2013.
